1.5

Copyright is a law put in place to stop people from stealing and using other peoples intellectual properties you can however use another persons property for commentary or review purposes. There are also other laws put in place that affect creating a website such as cookie law which affects the use of cookies on a site requiring permission to store cookie data and there is data protection to help keep user private information secure and encrypted.

1.6

When creating a website colour blindness should be taken to mind making sure text and background colours are clearly defined and non-obtrusive helping someone who might be colour blind to read all text you should also make sure your text is resizable so it makes it easier to read for people who have difficulty reading smaller text.

1.7

If uploading pictures, audio or video to your website you should make sure you are using a compatible file type such png, jpeg, gif or svg for pictures, or mp4, avi, mpg and ogg for videos, Then for audio you have wav, mp3, aac and many more for all types of media.
